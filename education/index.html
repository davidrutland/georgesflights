School report from Roscoe Council School, Garsfield Road, Senior Boys

George Heritage is a scholar in the 4th class (Std 7 A section). He was 14 years of age on Sept. 4th 1934. His conduct is very good and he is an intelligent worker. He is willing, obedient and has been a capable house captain, monitor, and has always been found honest & trustworthy. (Signed) WO Delaney

Roscoe School is still standing in 2022 - although there is no ‘Senior Boys’ section. The building was hit by a bomb during WW2.
